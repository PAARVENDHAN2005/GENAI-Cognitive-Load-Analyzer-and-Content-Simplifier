About GenAI Analyzer 🚀

Who We Are

GenAI Analyzer is an intelligent web application designed to simplify complex text, analyze cognitive load, and provide meaningful insights using AI-inspired logic.

What We Offer

🔹 Text Analysis: Measure words, sentences, and complexity.

🔹 AI Simplification: Convert complex text into simple language.

🔹 Sentiment Detection: Identify tone (positive / negative).

🔹 Student Grade Analyzer: Smart feedback based on performance.

🔹 History Tracking: Store and review past analyses.

Our Mission 🎯

Our goal is to make information easier to understand by combining artificial intelligence with simple user-friendly design. We aim to help students, professionals, and learners improve clarity and productivity.

Why This Project?

This project was built to demonstrate how AI can assist in everyday tasks like reading, writing, and analyzing text. It focuses on both functionality and user experience.
